Getting Started with TaskMaster

Welcome to TaskMaster! This guide will help you get started with our task management application and make the most of its features.

1. Creating an Account

  • Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage
  • Fill in your email address and create a secure password
  • Verify your email address to activate your account
  • Log in with your credentials
Note: Keep your password secure and never share it with anyone.

2. Managing Your Tasks

Adding a New Task

  • Click the "Add Task" button in the dashboard
  • Enter the task title and description
  • Set a priority level (High, Medium, Low)
  • Choose a due date
  • Click "Add Task" to save

Editing Tasks

  • Click the edit icon on any task card
  • Modify the task details as needed
  • Click "Save Changes" to update

Deleting Tasks

  • Click the delete icon on the task card
  • Confirm the deletion in the popup
Tip: Use the priority levels to organize your tasks effectively. High priority tasks will be highlighted in red.

3. Task Organization

Sorting Tasks

  • Use the "Sort by Priority" button to arrange tasks by importance
  • Use the "Sort by Date" button to organize tasks by due date

Filtering Tasks

  • Use the priority filter to show specific priority levels
  • Use the status filter to show completed or pending tasks

4. Task Status

  • Click the status button on a task to mark it as complete
  • Completed tasks will be moved to the bottom of the list
  • You can toggle the status back to pending if needed

5. User Interface

Dark Mode

  • The application automatically switches to dark mode based on your system preferences
  • Dark mode reduces eye strain in low-light conditions

Responsive Design

  • TaskMaster works on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile
  • The interface automatically adjusts to your screen size
